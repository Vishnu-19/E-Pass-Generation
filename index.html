Steps to Apply

The following are the steps to apply for the e-Pass. This application is completley automated.

Click on Apply Here

Fill out the Form and details for the e-Pass

Wait for the Application Process

The application process usually takes not more than 2 mins to be processed. Applicants who are allowed will have the option to download the e-pass

Download the Application

Note: The application is completley based on various factors and these will be considered to for approval or rejection of the e-Pass
